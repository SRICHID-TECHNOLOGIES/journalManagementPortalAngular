IWWA

IWWA was founded in 1968 with headquarters in Mumbai. It is a voluntary body of professionals concerned and connected with water supply for municipal, industrial, agricultural uses and treatment and disposal of wastewater. IWWA focuses basically on the entire Water Cycle" encompassing the environmental, social, institutional, and financing issues. IWWA has 35 Centres spread across the manuscript and is very active in conducting different activities in the areas of Water Supply and Wasterwater Treatment and Disposal. IWWA has a membership of more than over 21000 professionals spread all over the manuscript and abroad.
